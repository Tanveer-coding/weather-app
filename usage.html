How To Use This Weather App

Step 1

Open the main dashboard page of the weather application.

Step 2

Enter the name of any city in the search box.

Step 3

Click the Search button to fetch live weather data.

Step 4

View temperature, humidity, wind speed and sunrise/sunset.

Step 5

Check weather data of other cities in the table below.

Information Displayed

  • Current Temperature
  • Minimum and Maximum Temperature
  • Humidity
  • Wind Speed
  • Sunrise and Sunset Time
  • Cloud Percentage
